This Referral Program Service Agreement (“Services Agreement”) sets forth the terms and conditions of your use of this BitVez Referral Program (“Referral Program”) and serves to supplement the User Agreement between you and BitVez. In this Agreement “you”, “your”, or “referrer” refer to you or any agent, employee, servant or person authorized to act or operate your account on your behalf. “We”, “us” and “our” refer to BitVez.   

1 Description of the Referral Program

1.1 To enroll and participate in the Referral Program, you must be an existing user of BitVez with an active and functioning BitVez account, subject to and in compliance with user-related policies and requirements of BitVez, such as the User Agreement.  

1.2 This Referral Program provides you the opportunities of advertising and promoting BitVez via your own “website” or “publishing location” (such as an electronic newsletter or blog), thereby driving traffic to BitVez web site or web site content, whereby you may earn a portion of the revenue generated (“Commission”) if a person (“Visitor”) opens an account and trades a BitVez listed product after being referred to the BitVez web site from an internet hyperlink (“Link”) contained on your web site or publishing location.

1.3 After being referred to the BitVez website from a Link contained on your web site or publishing location, such Visitors have seven (7) days to complete their registration in order for your accounts to be linked. Should the Visitor click on another web site or publishing location’s Link that is not controlled by you or enter BitVez web site via another source-coded advertising Link shared by BitVez itself or another third party during the initial seven (7) days, your link will be overwritten, and you will not receive a Commission for that Visitor’s account.

1.4 You are not eligible for a Commission or any trading fee discounts on trades made by different accounts owned by yourself or accounts that are obviously owned by the same ultimate beneficiary.  

1.5 You are also not eligible for any Commission derived from any persons referred to the Platform by relying on any materials, videos, content, deliverables, work product owned by or licensed to BitVez (such as its research, blog or social media materials) linked, published, used, exploited or posted on your web site or publishing location.
