Q&A

What is a Buy / Sell Service?
Buy crypto services allow users to purchase cryptocurrencies with various payment methods such as debit/credit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, ACH, SEPA, PIX, etc., using their local fiat currencies.

Sell crypto services allow users to sell cryptocurrencies and receive their local fiat currencies in return.
Are there any fees associated with the Buy and Sell service?
The service fees vary depending on the fiat payment gateways and payment methods used. Please refer to our partners’ order details page for specific fee information.
What are the fiat payment gateways?
Fiat payment gateways are third-party providers that partner with Calvin-Bitrade to offer various payment methods for customers to buy and sell cryptocurrencies using their local currencies.

Currently, our partners include Banxa, Moonpay, Simplex, Alchemy Pay and Legend Trading.
I have more questions. How can I get additional help?
If you have more questions please refer to the Banxa FAQ、MoonPay FAQ 、Simplex FAQ、Alchemy Pay FAQ or Legend Trading FAQpage for more frequently asked questions and answers, including customer support information.
